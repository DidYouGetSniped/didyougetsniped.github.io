History

Founding & Merger

Both squads, IND and SSR, were on the brink of destruction. So to save themselves and their squads, they merged into one squad, MCF — bigger and better than the last two. The squad became an active member of the community, participating in squad wars, recruiting members, and overall prospering as an excellent squad.

Fall From Greatness

The squad started out as an active, popular War Brokers squad, but eventually people became permanently inactive. The leader and veteran, KillerFriend, left his leadership to join HellFire. The squad fell more and more as the remaining members went inactive and interest in it ceased. AriusXV was recruited and made into a new leader, but with that, Praneel and Kindness left. The squad was near destruction, so the remaining leaders came up with a plan to save the dying squad.

Decline & Disbandment

MCF continued to die as leaders Praneel, KillerFriend, and Kindness left the squad. It was left to AriusXV and The WW Wizard to revive it. For many months they sent recruitment messages and tried to get more people to join. A few months later, a new squad was made called Super Soldier Recruitment, or SSR. It was made by one of the original members of the unofficial SSR, Agent Broker. He claimed that it was the new SSR, saying that he was attempting to rebuild the squad, even though it still existed as MCF. Many community members were very unhappy with the new squad's name, but the mods decided to let them keep it. MCF now remained a dying squad with little hope for survival, and a new SSR was formed against the protests of the community.

As it stood, SSR was still a squad which hadn't yet turned out to be bad, except that Agent Broker left right after they finished making it. MCF was now completely dead, its members gone, and it had been officially disbanded. So there marks the official end of the old SSR, the official end of INDIAN, and now the official end of MCF.
