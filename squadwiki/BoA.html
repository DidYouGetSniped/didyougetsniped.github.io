History

Founding

Once upon a time on March 2, 2021, an experienced War Brokers player named Panzer saw that there was no designated place or squad for active players or War Brokers veterans who wanted to retire but still stay in the community, so he created the Beauty of Annihilation squad. It was a place where veterans and experienced players could stay active and have fun while attending to everyone else's needs, such as training the next generation of players. Codenamed Project BoA, this squad had the intention of changing the WB community for the better.
