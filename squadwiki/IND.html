History

Thoughts

A player named [INDIAN]BadBoyX had the thought of creating [INDIAN]. For the time being, he kept [INDIAN] as an unofficial squad. Then he joined the War Brokers server and talked to old friends like RIPPER and VoLcA.

The Thought Became a Desire

BadBoyX talked with RIPPER and thought of forming an official squad, [INDIAN]. RIPPER was a member of LTS at the time, the former Academy squad of STRIKE, which is known as BLIND. BadBoyX reached level 60 and formed the squad with RIPPER, Avaneesh, and Guy.

Journey

INDIAN was a quite successful squad because all India-based squads were dead. INDIAN had around 12 members, and everything was going extremely well.

The Fight

Two members of the squad, Ultro Hiru and Sharviesh, started fighting for the co-leader post after RIPPER had left. This fight caused other members to leave.

Aftermath

Eventually, everyone left and INDIAN disbanded. Everyone joined other squads: BadBoyX joined HKN, Hiru and Sharviesh joined LMS, LMTHEBOLD joined DEATH, and RIPPER remained squadless.

A Thought Again of Making INDIAN

BadBoyX had been in quite a few squads before having this thought again, including HKN, HF, BoA, AOS, and HP, before returning to BoA. He stayed in BoA for a long time, then thought of making INDIAN again.

Thoughts Change to Desire

BadBoyX talked with RIPPER and re-made INDIAN with the tag IND. It was going quite well, and IND joined the PA alliance consisting of BoA and SSR.

The Merge

BadBoyX thought that IND would not get as many members, so he asked Kindness about a possible merge with SSR. The merge happened, and IND is currently known as MCF. BadBoyX is currently known as Praneel, and RIPPER as Effect.
