Squad Inviter

Quickly invite players to your squad using their UID and game token

⚠️ Make sure to allow pop-ups for this page

Endpoint: https://store1.warbrokers.io/303/invite_squad_member.php?token=…&uid=…

How to Get Your Token

  1. Open warbrokers.io and log in to your account.
  2. Right-click anywhere on the page and select Inspect (or press Ctrl+Shift+I on Windows/Linux, Cmd+Option+I on Mac).
  3. Navigate to the Console tab in the developer tools.
  4. Type getCookie("game_token") and press Enter.
    Chrome tip: If pasting is blocked, type allow pasting first and press Enter, then try again.
  5. Copy the token value that appears and paste it into the Game Token field above.
⚠️ Security Warning

Keep your game token private — it grants full access to your War Brokers account.

After inviting squad members, log out from War Brokers to reset your token and ensure account security.
