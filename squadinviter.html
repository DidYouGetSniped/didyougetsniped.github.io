How to get your game token

  1. Open warbrokers.io and log in.
  2. Right‑click the page and choose Inspect (or press Ctrl+Shift+I on Windows/Linux, Cmd+Option+I on Mac).
  3. Go to the Console tab.
  4. Type getCookie("game_token") and press Enter.
    Chrome tip: if pasting is blocked, type allow pasting and press Enter, then paste again.
  5. Copy the value that appears and paste it into the Game token field above.

WARNING: Keep your token private — it grants access to your account.

After you're done inviting squad members, log out from your War Brokers account to reset your game token and ensure security.
